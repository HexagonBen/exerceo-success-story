Ben’s Success Story

Ben came to Exerceo with no computer science degree, and no professional experience as a software engineer. He had been a registered nurse for 5 years, and decided to make a career change.

Although Ben's resume carried nothing relevant to software engineering aside from a handful of certificates from a non-accredited online bootcamp, Exerceo gave Ben a supportive environment to learn in, and the chance to showcase what he did bring to the table already: a positive attitude, reliability, and a ravenous appetite for learning. Over the course of a few months, Ben progressed through Exerceo's rigorous immersion program.

Ben's career is just getting started, but with Exerceo's help, Ben is well on his way to developing himself into an valuable asset.
